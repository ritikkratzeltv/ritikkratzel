    Landyachtz

    Landyachtz is known as one of the best cruiser board manufacturer in the world, and I can see why! Based in Canada, they truly excel in creating top-quality skateboards that perform like a dream. I want to give a big shoutout to Alex and the entire Landyachtz team for their incredible generosity. They sent me three fantastic boards—all the way from Canada—two ATV Perfecto models and one Classic Dinghy, along with extra wheels, bearings, and some cool t-shirts! They’ve also been kind enough to share my story and give me shoutouts on their social media platforms, helping me reach a wider audience. I’m proud to say I’m now one of their brand ambassadors. If you’re looking for high-quality gear that stands out, I highly recommend checking out Landyachtz. You can even get 10% off your next purchase using this link.

    No More

    As someone who is environmentally conscious and promotes a lower carbon footprint through long-distance skateboarding, I truly resonate with the mission of No More. This incredible zero-waste brand creates all of their bags and accessories using reclaimed materials, demonstrating a commitment to sustainability that aligns perfectly with my values. They recently sent me one of their unique bags, and I absolutely love it! Each product is crafted to be one-of-a-kind, ensuring that your purchase is not just stylish but also beneficial for the planet. I’m excited for future collaborations, including the possibility of creating a skate bag together!

    Foxyroks

    The man behind the art at Foxyroks has also become a personal friend. Based in UK, he goes out of his way to support me and is actively trying to find sponsors for my future trips. His creations are truly a form of art; whether it’s jewelry, lamps, knives, or clocks, everything is made from recycled skateboards, showcasing both creativity and sustainability. Each piece not only tells a story but also reflects a commitment to reducing waste and celebrating the skateboarding culture we love. I’m continually inspired by the craftsmanship and ingenuity that goes into every item. Mate, I truly appreciate your support!
