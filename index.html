    Skate Across India

    During my solo skateboarding journey from the mountains of Manali to the southernmost tip of India in Kanyakumari, I had no idea what I’d encounter. But what I found was a world of kindness. Within days, strangers welcomed me with open arms—shopkeepers, police officers, and even politicians invited me into their homes, treating me like family. On the road, I was often handed local snacks, from piping hot chai to fresh fruits and street food, which fueled my days. Of course, the journey had its fair share of challenges: scorching heat, rough roads, and relentless dogs that seemed to appear at every corner. Yet, every struggle only made the experience more unforgettable. I wouldn’t change a single moment.
